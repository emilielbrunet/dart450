You made it!

What was that all about? You just let yourself get bullied into doing a bunch of weird things. But you kinda wanted to see what would come next, and wasn’t it fun? That’s because of the power of interactivity my friend. This website was made as an experiment into how multisensorial interactivity can engage with users to evoke emotional responses and encourage prolonged participation. So thank you for allowing yourself to momentarily step outside of the conventions of web design and experience something new online.
